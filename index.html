How FuelFlow works

1️⃣
Odd / Even rule
Your number plate’s last digit decides everything.
Ends in 1,3,5,7,9 → only odd dates (1,3,5…)
Ends in 0,2,4,6,8 → only even dates (2,4,6…)
2️⃣
Weekly limit resets every Saturday
You get ONE fill-up per week.
No matter what day you filled this week, the limit resets at Saturday.
If you already filled → we calculate from the next Saturday onward.
3️⃣
Car = 15 L • Bike = 5 L
Limits shown exactly as per government announcement.

FuelFlow automatically finds the soonest allowed date using today’s real date in Sri Lanka time. No forms to submit — it updates live while you type!
